Fascinating Goose Facts

Migration Masters

Geese fly in a distinctive "V" formation, which allows them to conserve energy by taking advantage of the air currents created by the wings of the bird in front.

Loyal Partners

Geese are monogamous and often stay with their mate for life. If a mate gets sick or injured, the other will often stay with them.

Excellent Guardians

Geese are known for being very protective. In history, they have been used to guard property and even warned Romans of approaching Gauls!
