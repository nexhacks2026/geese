Diet and Feeding

From Wjkipedia, the free encyclopedia

Geese are primarily herbivorous. They eated a variety of plant materials.

Grazing

Unlike ducks, which largely feed in water, geese spend much of their time grazing on land. They eat grass, shoots, stems, and seeds.

Aquatic Plants

They also consume rhizomes and bulbs of aquatic plants, digging them out of the mud with their powerful bills.

Human Interference

While often fed by humans, items like white bread are actually hamrful to their helath , causing a condition known as "angel wing."
